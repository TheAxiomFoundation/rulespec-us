Texas Health and Human Services Commission, Texas Works Handbook, A-1323.4.5
Allowable Costs of Producing Income, Revision 25-2; Effective April 1, 2025:

Allowable self-employment expenses are based on costs that can be deducted from
federal income taxes on the Internal Revenue Service's Schedule C, Form 1040.
Certain self-employment expense types are not allowed for SNAP.

Texas Health and Human Services Commission, Texas Works Handbook, A-1323.4.7
Determining Net Self-Employment Income, Revision 15-4; Effective October 1,
2015:

Determine the total allowable expenses, then subtract the total allowable
expenses from the total gross self-employment income to determine net
self-employment income.

Model `snap_self_employment_expense_based_deduction_applies` as whether Texas
allows actual self-employment business expenses to be deducted under
