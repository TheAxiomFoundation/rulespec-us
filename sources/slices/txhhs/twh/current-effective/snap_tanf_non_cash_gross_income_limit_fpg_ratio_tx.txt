Texas Health and Human Services Commission, Texas Works Handbook, B-471
Eligibility Criteria, Revision 15-4; Effective October 1, 2015:

SNAP households meet categorical eligibility criteria if:
- all members are approved for TANF cash assistance or SSI; or
- the household:
  - meets the resource criteria to be authorized to receive TANF Non-cash
    (TANF-NC) services; and
  - has gross income less than or equal to 165 percent of the Federal Poverty
    Income Limit (FPIL) for its size.
